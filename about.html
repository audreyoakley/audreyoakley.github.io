Meet Audrey's Bakery

What began as a weekend baking hobby has grown into a warm neighborhood bakery dedicated to handcrafted pastries, caring service, and joyful celebrations.

Our Story

Audrey opened the bakery in 2013 with a simple goal: make every recipe better than the last. Today, we still bake with the same care and attention, using trusted ingredients and creativity.

Our Values

Community, quality, and kindness guide everything we do. We aim to be the bakery that brings your family together for special celebrations and cozy everyday treats.

Why Audrey's?

We blend classic techniques with modern favorites, and we love helping customers celebrate milestones with thoughtful, beautiful baked goods.
